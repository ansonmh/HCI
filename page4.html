Campuses

CCCU provides a wide range of facilities and resources to help to make your stay at CCCU a satisfying and enjoyable experience.

Kowloon Tong Campus

CCCU/UOWCHK occupies offices in the City University of Hong Kong’s Kowloon Tong Campus. AD Students enrolled in programmes at this campus can use the lecture theatres, classrooms, laboratories and other state-of-the-art teaching and learning facilities of the University. This campus is easily accessible, only a short walk from the MTR and East Rail stations at Kowloon Tong.

The Run Run Shaw Library City University

The Run Run Shaw Library, located at the City University campus, has around 2,518 seats. It supports the learning, study and research needs of the University and our AD students and staff. Students may borrow from a collection of more than 1,068,000 volumes of printed books, and around 219,600 volumes of bound periodicals. Students are also able to access an expanding number of electronic databases, e-journals and media resources.

Computing Services

The Computing Services Centre at the City University campus maintains host machines, teaching studio area, printing services, electronic mail, literacy programmes, express terminals, and a service counter. AD students have ready access to a wide range of electronic resources and to the technology which allows them to communicate with teachers and scholars both locally and internationally.
All computers in the Centre are automatically connected to the sophisticated CTNET. These can be used as workstations or as terminals for accessing software and services available at the Centre’s central computers.



Kowloon Bay
Telford Annex Campus

Our second campus also offers convenient access to students and is just minutes from the Kowloon Bay MTR station. This campus has up-to-date facilities for teaching and learning, as well as specially designed recreational space for an interactive campus life. AD students enrolled in programmes offered at the Telford campus can also use the library and computing facilities at the City University campus in Kowloon Tong. Students enrolled in the Diploma in General Studies programme at the Telford campus are entitled to use the Telford Library and the facilities at the Telford Recreation Club.



Telford Annex Library Outlet

The Telford campus has a well-equipped on-site Library, offering students easy access to textbooks, periodicals and library materials that support the programmes offered at the Telford Annex. Online facilities are available for connection to the electronic resources at the Run Run Shaw Library. AD students at Telford Annex can reserve, check out and return materials at both the Run Run Shaw Library and the Telford Annex Library Outlet.
