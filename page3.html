VISION AND MISSION

Vision

CCCU aspires to be Hong Kong's leading private non-profit university that prepares graduates for lifelong learning, educates future leaders and enriches lives.

Mission

As an Australian international tertiary institution CCCU is committed to excellence in teaching and learning and community engagement, serving Hong Kong and the region. CCCU seeks to nurture students' intellectual growth, creativity, integrity, and global outlook and empowers them to contribute their talents to society.

Values

Passion                                    We love what we do
Excellence                              We strive for excellence in all we do
Integrity                                   We are honest, ethical and reliable
Accountability                        We take ownership for what we do
Civility and Collegiality         We embrace mutual respect, diversity and equity

Graduate
Attributes

Upon completion of their studies, graduates of CCCU are expected to excel in disciplinary knowledge and to demonstrate high standards of personal integrity and civility. 


Graduates of CCCU should:

be eligible to articulate into senior years of bachelor degree programmes;

be able to demonstrate a required level of discipline-specific knowledge;

demonstrate preparedness for employment as para-professionals;

apply effective communication, language, numeracy, critical thinking and information technology skills in their pursuit of further academic and professional qualifications, as well as in the workplace;

work effectively and harmoniously with people from different social and cultural backgrounds;

act as ethically and socially responsible citizens in a globalizing society; and

embrace opportunities for lifelong learning.
