Community College of City University

The Community College of City University (“CCCU”) is well recognized as a premier college in Hong Kong providing quality tertiary education for about 6,000 students. CCCU offers a wide range of Associate Degree (AD) programmes, a Diploma in General Studies, and International Language Proficiency Tests with preparatory courses.

Its AD programmes are designed to complement a core area of specialization with substantial components in generic skills and general education which prepare students for senior years of university education and employment as well as nurture their personal development.


With about 170 well qualified and dedicated staff, CCCU is highly regarded for its rigor in quality assurance and its emphasis on holistic education. Significant resources are devoted to devising a rigorous curriculum and arranging overseas study tours and short courses, internships, and career exposure and mentorship programmes that facilitate the intellectual development of students. Positive feedback from employers who offer placement opportunities and internships affirms the role played by the College in the education, career development and personal growth of our students.

The majority of graduates progressing to further studies have either been able to articulate into senior-year places or admitted with advanced standing in undergraduate programmes (inclusive of self-funded programmes) offered by University Grants Committee(“UGC”)-funded institutions in Hong Kong.


Since July 2015, CCCU has been in a formal alliance with the University of Wollongong (UOW), Australia, through which our students’ horizons and learning experiences have been widened and enriched via exchange programmes to UOW. Articulation opportunities for our graduates have also been enhanced with the availability of UOW top-up degrees. 

As a result of this alliance, students admitted to the great majority of Associate Degree programmes at the College will, in addition to their CityU awards, be entitled to a locally accredited, internationally recognized qualification from UOW College Hong Kong, CCCU’s future name.
