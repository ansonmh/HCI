Message from the Principal

Welcome to Community College of City University (CCCU)/UOW College Hong Kong!

At CCCU, we attach much importance to delivering top quality education that not only nurtures the intellect, but also foster the personal discovery and growth through exposure and a wide range of student activities and services. Essential skills in communication, languages, numeracy and information technology are built into our curricula so that students are well equipped for further studies and employment. When you study at our College, you can expect an exciting campus life and learning experience.


The majority of our graduates from our Associate Degree (AD) programmes who progress to further studies have either been able to articulate into senior year places or admitted with advanced standing in undergraduate programmes (including self-funded programmes) offered by University Grants Committee (UGC)-funded institutions in Hong Kong. Our strategic alliance with the University of Wollongong in Australia since 2015 also offers unique opportunities for further studies in UOW undergraduate programmes. As a result of this alliance, students admitted to the great majority of our AD programmes will be entitled to dual awards, one from CityU and the other from UOW College Hong Kong (UOWCHK), CCCU's future name. The UOWCHK award is a locally accredited, internationally recognized qualification.


Apart from academic studies, you will have opportunities to gain invaluable exposure from a wide range of overseas study tours, internships, all designed to help learners actualize their full potentials. Our students enjoy the quality teaching, learning ambience, early career exposure and the many articulation opportunities into relevant undergraduate degree programmes. Our experienced teaching staff are devoted to provide you with pastoral care as well as academic guidance.


You are welcome to contact us should you require further assistance in application or selection of programmes. We hope you can find from our wide variety of programmes one that meets your aspirations. We look forward to welcoming you to the College in September 2018!

Jennifer Ng

Principal
Community College of City University/UOW College Hong Kong
