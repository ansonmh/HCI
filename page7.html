Message from Head

Welcome to Division of
Applied Science and Technology (AST)!

The Division of Applied Science and Technology (AST), with a vision of “Sustained Excellence in Applied Science and Technology Education in Hong Kong”, offers academic programmes in various fields of science and technology which enable the younger generation to experience the application of different technologies and help them prepare for further studies and career. These include academic programmes in the areas of information technology, aviation logistics, environmental studies, business analysis, engineering and creative media. Graduates of our AD programmes have good opportunities for further study at bachelor degree level, both locally and overseas.

Currently, the Division offers seven associate degree programmes and one top-up programme. Supported by experienced academic staff and with professional advices from industrial experts, programme curricula are designed to provide students with the basic principles, concepts and methods of applications in their chosen areas of study. The Division also possesses up-to-date facilities including state-of-the-art computer laboratories and equipment, network and multimedia laboratories and flight simulation facilities.

The Division organizes internships, field trips and joint student projects with various organizations, as well as visits and overseas study tours to Japan, Korea, Singapore, UK, US, Australia, New Zealand and elsewhere. Seminars, career talks, competitions and social gatherings are regularly held to enhance whole person development and peer learning among students.

Dr Kenny MA 

Head
