Student Activities

The Division works to maximize students’ learning opportunities not just beyond classroom but also beyond the territory. Locally, student activities like social gatherings, articulation seminars, field trips, etc. are organized. Internationally, there are the CCCU Study Abroad Scheme and different overseas study tours. Places visited include Munich, Vancouver, Singapore, Dubai, the United States of America, Korea, Taiwan, Japan, New Zealand etc.
